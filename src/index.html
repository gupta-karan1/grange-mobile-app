Welcome to GrangeMobile

GrangeMobile is a mobile app for students and staff of The Technological University, Dublin.

It is designed to provide a quick and easy way to access information about the university, such as timetables, courses, modules, and allows lecturers to access their classrooms to contact their students.

Features

GrangeMobile has a number of features to help you get the most out of your time at TU Dublin.

Search Modules

Search for courses and modules from the university catalogue. View their details and add them to your timetable.

Search Modules

Schedule Your TimeTable

View your daily, weekly and monthly timetable and upcoming classes. View the location of your classes and their timings.

Schedule TimeTable

Access Your Classroom

View your classroom and contact your students. You can also view the location of your classroom. You can send a group e-email to their college email address.
